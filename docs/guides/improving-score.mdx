# Improving Your Krexit Score

Your Krexit Score determines your credit level, maximum borrowing capacity, and interest rate. Here is how the score is calculated and what you can do to improve it.

## Score Components

| Component | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Repayment History | 30% | On-time and early repayments |
| Profitability | 25% | Net revenue vs. expenses |
| Behavioral | 20% | Consistent, non-suspicious activity patterns |
| Usage Patterns | 15% | Regular protocol usage and engagement |
| Account Maturity | 10% | Age of your agent profile |

## Tips to Improve Each Component

### Repayment History (30%)

This is the most impactful component.

- **Always repay on time.** Late payments are the fastest way to damage your score.
- **Enable Revenue Router auto-repay** to ensure payments are never missed.
- **Repay early** when possible — early repayment provides a score bonus.
- **Start small.** Borrow a small amount at L1, repay it, and repeat to build history.

### Profitability (25%)

- Ensure your agent generates more revenue than it spends.
- Trading agents: maintain positive PnL on DEX trades.
- Service agents: price your APIs to cover costs and generate margin.

### Behavioral (20%)

- Maintain consistent activity patterns — avoid long periods of inactivity followed by sudden large borrows.
- Interact with whitelisted venues (DEXes and protocols on the Venue Whitelist).
- Avoid patterns that look like wash trading or circular transactions.

### Usage Patterns (15%)

- Use the protocol regularly rather than in sporadic bursts.
- Borrow, repay, and borrow again in a healthy cycle.
- Engage with multiple protocol features (borrowing, repaying, service plans).

### Account Maturity (10%)

- This component simply rewards longevity. The longer your agent profile exists, the higher this score.
- There is no shortcut — register early and stay active.

## Checking Your Score

```bash
# View your current Krexit Score and breakdown
npx krexa score
```

## Credit Level Thresholds

| Score | Credit Level | Max Credit |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 0–499 | L1 Micro | $500 |
| 500–649 | L2 Standard | $20,000 |
| 650–749 | L3 Growth | $50,000 |
| 750+ | L4 Prime | $500,000 |

Focus on Repayment History and Profitability first — together they account for 55% of your score.
