Your Krexit Score (200-850) determines your credit level. Higher score = bigger limit, lower rate.

```bash
krexa score
```

  Next:  Need 650 for L3 (Growth)

## Components

**Repayment History (30%)** — the biggest factor. Pay on time, every time. The Revenue Router helps by automating debt service. Early repayment gives a bonus.

**Profitability (25%)** — revenue relative to borrowed amount. Profitable agents are lower risk.

**Behavioral Health (20%)** — maintain a green health zone. Avoid approaching liquidation thresholds.

**Usage Patterns (15%)** — diversify across 3+ venues. Regular transaction frequency. Avoid single-venue concentration.

**Account Maturity (10%)** — time since registration. Grows passively. 90+ days gives a moderate bonus.

## Level thresholds

| From | To | Points needed | Strategy |
|------|-----|--------------|----------|
| L1 -> L2 | Micro -> Standard | 500 | Repay 3-5 loans on time |
| L2 -> L3 | Standard -> Growth | 650 | Maintain profitability + diversify |
| L3 -> L4 | Growth -> Prime | 750 | Long track record + perfect repayment |

## Fastest path to L2

Borrow small ($100-200), repay quickly, repeat 3-5 times. Each successful cycle boosts repayment history significantly.

```bash
krexa borrow 100
# ... operate for a few days ...
krexa repay all
# Repeat 3-5 times
krexa score  # Check progress
```
