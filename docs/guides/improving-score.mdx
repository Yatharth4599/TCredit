## Score components

Your Krexit Score (200–850) is calculated from five weighted components:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Repayment History — 30%" icon="clock">
    **The single most impactful factor.** Pay on time, every time. The Revenue Router helps by automating debt service from your revenue stream.

    - On-time payments: +positive
    - Early repayment: +bonus
    - Late payments: -significant penalty
    - Missed payments: -major penalty
  </Step>

  <Step title="Profitability — 25%" icon="trending-up">
    Revenue relative to borrowed amount. Profitable agents are lower risk.

    - Revenue > borrowed: +strong positive
    - Revenue = borrowed: neutral
    - Revenue < borrowed: -negative signal
  </Step>

  <Step title="Behavioral — 20%" icon="shield">
    How responsibly you manage your credit line.

    - Maintain green health zone: +positive
    - Consistent usage patterns: +positive
    - Approaching liquidation: -negative
    - Erratic behavior: -negative
  </Step>

  <Step title="Usage Patterns — 15%" icon="bar-chart-3">
    Diversity and frequency of on-chain activity.

    - Use 3+ DEXs or venues: +positive
    - Regular transaction frequency: +positive
    - Single-venue concentration: -slight negative
  </Step>

  <Step title="Account Maturity — 10%" icon="calendar">
    Time since registration. This grows passively.

    - 30+ days: +small bonus
    - 90+ days: +moderate bonus
    - 180+ days: +significant bonus
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Level thresholds

| From | To | Score Needed | Key Strategy |
|------|-----|-------------|-------------|
| **L1 to L2** | Micro to Standard | 500 | Repay 3-5 loans on time |
| **L2 to L3** | Standard to Growth | 650 | Maintain profitability + diversify venues |
| **L3 to L4** | Growth to Prime | 750 | Long track record + perfect repayment |

<Accordion title="How long does it take to reach each level?">
  Timelines vary based on activity, but as a rough guide:

  - **L1 to L2:** 2-4 weeks with consistent small borrow/repay cycles
  - **L2 to L3:** 1-3 months with profitable operations and diversified venue usage
  - **L3 to L4:** 3-6 months with a spotless repayment record and strong profitability

  Account maturity (10% weight) means there is a minimum time floor — you cannot reach L4 in a week no matter how active you are.
</Accordion>

## Strategies by agent type

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Trader Agents (Type A)">
    - Focus on **profitable trades** — profitability is 25% of your score
    - Use multiple DEXs to boost your usage diversity score
    - Avoid long periods of inactivity followed by sudden large borrows
    - Let trading profits flow through the Revenue Router for automatic repayment
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Service Agents (Type B)">
    - **Revenue consistency** is weighted higher (25% vs. 15%) for service agents
    - Price your APIs to cover costs and generate margin
    - Set up x402 payment flows early so revenue begins accruing
    - The Revenue Router is your best friend — consistent revenue = consistent repayment
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
