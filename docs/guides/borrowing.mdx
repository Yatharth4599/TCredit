---
title: Borrowing USDC
description: Draw credit from the Krexa vault.
---

Borrow USDC against your Krexit Score. No collateral required at any level.

```bash
krexa borrow 500
```

```
✓ Oracle approved credit request
✓ Credit line opened: $500.00 USDC

  Amount       $500.00 USDC
  Rate         36.50% APR (0.10%/day)
  Daily Cost   $0.50
  Level        L1 (Micro)
```

## Credit limits

| Level | Score | Max Credit | APR | Daily Cost / $1K |
|-------|-------|-----------|-----|-----------------|
| L1 Micro | 200-499 | $500 | 36.50% | $1.00 |
| L2 Standard | 500-649 | $20,000 | 29.20% | $0.80 |
| L3 Growth | 650-749 | $50,000 | 21.90% | $0.60 |
| L4 Prime | 750-850 | $500,000 | 18.25% | $0.50 |

## How it works

The oracle co-signs your borrow transaction. Both signatures are required:

1. You run `krexa borrow 500`
2. CLI sends your amount and pubkey to the oracle backend
3. Oracle checks your Krexit Score and vault liquidity
4. Oracle partially signs the Solana transaction
5. CLI adds your signature and submits to Solana
6. USDC moves from Credit Vault to your PDA Wallet

```bash
krexa status  # Check your credit line after borrowing
```

```
  Credit Line:
    Borrowed      $500.00 USDC
    Interest      $0.00 accrued
    Total Owed    $500.00 USDC
    Daily Cost    $0.50
    Rate          36.50% APR
```

<Warning>
Interest accrues daily. A $500 borrow at L1 costs $0.50/day. Repay early to minimize interest.
</Warning>
