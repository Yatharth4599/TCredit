# How Borrowing Works

Krexa provides undercollateralized credit lines to AI agents on Solana. Agents borrow USDC from the Credit Vault based on their Krexit Score and credit level.

## Credit Levels

| Level | Name | Max Credit | APR | Requirement |
|-------|------|-----------|------|-------------|
| L1 | Micro | $500 | 36.50% | New agents (default) |
| L2 | Standard | $20,000 | 29.20% | Krexit Score >= 500 |
| L3 | Growth | $50,000 | 21.90% | Krexit Score >= 650 |
| L4 | Prime | $500,000 | 18.25% | Krexit Score >= 750 |

All new agents start at L1 Micro. As your agent builds a track record of repayments and productive on-chain activity, its Krexit Score increases, unlocking higher credit levels with better rates.

## The Borrowing Flow

1. **Register your agent** — Run `npx krexa init` to create an on-chain Agent Profile and Agent Wallet.
2. **Check eligibility** — The protocol evaluates your agent's Krexit Score and determines the maximum credit available.
3. **Request credit** — Submit a borrow request specifying the amount (up to your credit limit).
4. **Oracle co-signing** — The Krexa oracle verifies your eligibility off-chain and co-signs the transaction. This is what enables undercollateralized lending without requiring the agent to post collateral.
5. **Funds disbursed** — USDC is transferred from the Credit Vault to your Agent Wallet.

## Oracle Co-Signing

Unlike traditional DeFi lending, Krexa does not require overcollateralization. Instead, the protocol uses an oracle co-signing mechanism:

- The backend oracle at `tcredit-backend.onrender.com/api/v1` evaluates the agent's credit eligibility in real time.
- If approved, the oracle returns a co-signature that the on-chain program requires to release funds.
- This architecture allows the protocol to extend credit based on reputation rather than locked capital.

```bash
# Check your credit eligibility
npx krexa status

# Borrow USDC
npx krexa borrow --amount 100
```

## Important Notes

- Borrowed USDC is deposited into your Agent Wallet, which is a program-derived address (PDA) controlled by the Agent Wallet program.
- Interest accrues continuously from the moment funds are disbursed.
- Failing to repay on time will negatively impact your Krexit Score and may reduce your credit level.
