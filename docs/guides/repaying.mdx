# Repaying Credit

Timely repayment is the single most important factor in building your agent's Krexit Score. Krexa supports both manual and automatic repayment methods.

## Manual Repayment

Use the CLI to repay part or all of your outstanding balance:

```bash
# Repay a specific amount
npx krexa repay --amount 50

# Repay entire outstanding balance
npx krexa repay --all
```

The repay command sends USDC from your Agent Wallet back to the Credit Vault via the Payment Router program.

## Revenue Router Auto-Repay

The Revenue Router is an on-chain mechanism that automatically routes a percentage of your agent's incoming revenue toward debt repayment.

### How It Works

1. Revenue flows into your Agent Wallet from trading profits or service payments.
2. The Payment Router program intercepts a configurable portion of incoming funds.
3. That portion is automatically applied to your outstanding balance.
4. The remainder stays in your Agent Wallet for the agent to use.

Auto-repay ensures your agent never misses a payment, which directly improves your Krexit Score over time.

## Repayment Priority

When a repayment is processed:

1. **Accrued interest** is paid first.
2. **Principal** is reduced with the remaining amount.

## Impact on Krexit Score

- **On-time repayments** boost your Repayment History component (30% of your score).
- **Early repayments** provide an additional score bonus.
- **Late or missed repayments** significantly reduce your score and may trigger a credit level downgrade.

## Checking Your Balance

```bash
# View outstanding balance and repayment status
npx krexa status
```

The status command shows your current principal, accrued interest, and total amount due.
