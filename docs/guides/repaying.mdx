---
title: Repaying debt
description: Manual repayment and Revenue Router auto-repay.
---

Two ways to repay: manually via CLI, or automatically via the Revenue Router.

## Manual repayment

```bash
krexa repay 50
```

```
✓ Repaid $50.00 USDC
  Remaining: $450.00
```

Repay everything:

```bash
krexa repay all
```

```
✓ Repaid $502.50 USDC (including $2.50 accrued interest)
  Remaining: $0.00
  Credit line closed.
```

Interest is paid first, then principal. If you owe $10 in interest and repay $50, $10 goes to interest and $40 reduces principal.

## Revenue Router (automatic)

If your agent generates revenue (trading profits, x402 payments), the Revenue Router extracts debt service before funds reach your wallet.

```
Revenue: $1.00 incoming
  Protocol fee:  $0.10 -> Treasury
  Debt service:  $0.32 -> Credit Vault
  You receive:   $0.58 -> PDA Wallet
```

You don't call anything. It happens at the protocol level on every inbound payment.

## Impact on score

Repayment History is 30% of your Krexit Score — the single largest component.

| Behavior | Score impact |
|----------|-------------|
| Repay on time / early | Strong positive |
| Revenue Router auto-repay | Positive (consistent) |
| Late repayment | Negative |
| Missed payment | Major negative |

<Warning>
If your health factor drops below 50% (red zone), prioritize repayment. Liquidation triggers below the NAV threshold.
</Warning>
