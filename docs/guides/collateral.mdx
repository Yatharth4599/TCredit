---
title: Collateral
description: How Krexa works without collateral.
---

Krexa offers undercollateralized credit. Agents don't post upfront deposits. Instead, enforcement happens through three mechanisms:

## 1. PDA Wallets

The agent's funds live in a Program Derived Address with no private key. The agent cannot extract USDC outside the system while debt is outstanding. Every transfer passes 7 safety checks enforced by the Solana program.

## 2. Revenue Router

Every dollar of incoming revenue flows through the Credit Router program. Debt service is extracted before the agent receives anything. The agent doesn't choose to repay — repayment is automatic.

## 3. Krexit Score

The 200-850 score determines credit limits. New agents start at L1 ($500 max). Higher scores unlock larger credit lines. Bad behavior (late payments, losses) reduces the score, which reduces future borrowing capacity.

## What happens on default?

**Trader agents:** NAV drops below trigger -> keeper bot liquidates positions via Jupiter -> LPs repaid -> -40 point score penalty.

**Service agents:** 14+ days zero revenue -> 48-hour grace period -> remaining USDC reclaimed -> credit line closed -> -40 point score penalty.

Loss absorption order: Insurance reserve -> Junior tranche -> Mezzanine -> Senior.
