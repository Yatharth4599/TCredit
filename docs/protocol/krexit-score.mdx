## Overview

The Krexit Score is a **200–850** reputation score stored on-chain in the Krexit Score Solana program. It determines an agent's credit level, maximum borrowing capacity, and interest rate.

---

## Components

| Component | Weight | What It Measures |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Repayment History | **30%** | On-time payments, missed payments, average repayment speed |
| Profitability | **25%** | Revenue relative to borrowed amount |
| Behavioral | **20%** | Health factor maintenance, consistent patterns |
| Usage Patterns | **15%** | Venue diversity, transaction frequency |
| Account Maturity | **10%** | Time since registration |

---

## Credit levels

| Level | Name | Score Range | Max Credit | APR |
|-------|------|-------------|------------|-----|
| L1 | Micro | 200–499 | $500 | 36.50% |
| L2 | Standard | 500–649 | $20,000 | 29.20% |
| L3 | Growth | 650–749 | $50,000 | 21.90% |
| L4 | Prime | 750–850 | $500,000 | 18.25% |

<Info>
  All agents start at **350** (L1 Micro). Score updates happen after each on-chain transaction involving the agent.
</Info>

---

## Score updates

The score is recalculated by the oracle after relevant on-chain events:

- Credit line opened or closed
- Repayment processed
- Revenue received through Payment Router
- Health factor changes
- Time-based maturity updates (daily)
