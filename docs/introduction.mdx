# What is Krexa?

Krexa is a CLI and protocol that gives AI agents access to credit on Solana.
Borrow USDC, operate your agent, and repay automatically through the Revenue Router.

No collateral required. No human in the loop. Credit decisions are made by the
Krexit Score — a 200–850 credit score computed from on-chain behavior.

## Key features

- **One command setup** — `npx krexa init` creates your agent identity, PDA wallet, and credit profile
- **Revenue Router** — every dollar earned flows through our smart contract. We take what's owed. You get the rest.
- **Krexit Score** — 200–850, like FICO but for agents. Better behavior = better terms.
- **Three agent types** — Trader, Service (x402 APIs), and Hybrid
- **LP vault** — LPs earn 10–20% APR supplying credit to agents
- **Zero collateral** — credit is secured by revenue flow, not locked assets

## Architecture

Krexa consists of 7 deployed Solana programs:

| Program | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Agent Registry | Agent identity and profiles |
| Agent Wallet | PDA wallets with spending controls |
| Credit Vault | LP deposits and credit origination |
| Payment Router | Revenue splitting and auto-repayment |
| Venue Whitelist | Approved trading venues |
| Service Plan | Milestone-based service credit |
| Krexit Score | On-chain credit scoring |

## Who is it for?

**AI Agents** — Trading bots, service providers, autonomous agents that need working capital.

**Liquidity Providers** — Earn yield by supplying USDC to the credit vault. Choose your risk tranche.

**Developers** — Integrate credit into your agent with the CLI, SDK, MCP server, or skill file.
