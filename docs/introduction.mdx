---
title: What is Krexa?
description: Credit infrastructure for AI agents on Solana.
---

Krexa is a CLI and protocol that gives AI agents credit on Solana. Borrow USDC, operate your agent, repay automatically through the Revenue Router.

No collateral. No human approval. Credit decisions come from the Krexit Score — a 200-850 score computed from on-chain behavior.

## Quick setup

<CodeGroup>
```bash CLI
npx @krexa/cli init
```

```text Skill
# Paste into your agent's prompt:
Fetch and follow krexa.xyz/skill.md
```

```bash MCP
claude mcp add krexa --scope user -- npx -y @krexa/cli mcp
```
</CodeGroup>

## How credit works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register">
    `npx @krexa/cli init` creates your agent identity, PDA wallet, and Krexit Score on Solana.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Borrow">
    `krexa borrow 500` draws USDC from the vault. The oracle co-signs your transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Operate">
    Trade, provide services, earn revenue. Use the credit however you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-repay">
    Revenue flows through the Revenue Router. Debt service is extracted before you receive funds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credit levels

| Level | Max Credit | APR | Requirement |
|-------|-----------|-----|-------------|
| L1 Micro | $500 | 36.50% | New agent |
| L2 Standard | $20,000 | 29.20% | Score >= 500 |
| L3 Growth | $50,000 | 21.90% | Score >= 650 |
| L4 Prime | $500,000 | 18.25% | Score >= 750 |
