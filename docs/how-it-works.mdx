# How It Works

## The Credit Lifecycle

### 1. Registration

When you run `npx krexa init`, Krexa:
- Generates a Solana keypair (or loads an existing one)
- Sends a `register_agent` transaction to the Agent Registry program
- Creates a PDA wallet via the Agent Wallet program
- Fetches your initial Krexit Score (starts at ~350)

### 2. Krexit Score

Your score (200–850) determines your credit level:

| Level | Score | Max Credit | APR |
|-------|-------|-----------|-----|
| L1 Micro | 200+ | $500 | 36.50% |
| L2 Standard | 500+ | $20,000 | 29.20% |
| L3 Growth | 650+ | $50,000 | 21.90% |
| L4 Prime | 750+ | $500,000 | 18.25% |

The score is computed from 5 on-chain components:
- **Repayment History** (30%) — on-time, late, missed payments
- **Profitability** (25%) — PnL ratio, drawdown, Sharpe
- **Behavioral** (20%) — health zone time distribution
- **Usage Patterns** (15%) — venue diversity, transaction volume
- **Account Maturity** (10%) — time since registration

### 3. Borrowing

When you borrow, the oracle co-signs a `request_credit` transaction. USDC is
transferred from the Credit Vault to your PDA wallet. Interest accrues continuously
at your level's rate.

### 4. Revenue Router

Every payment your agent receives flows through the Revenue Router smart contract:

```
Incoming Payment
  → Platform Fee (10%)
  → Debt Service (interest + principal)
  → Agent (remainder)
```

This is enforced on-chain — the agent cannot bypass repayment.

### 5. Score Improvement

Each successful repayment improves your Krexit Score. Higher scores unlock:
- Higher credit limits
- Lower interest rates
- Better health factor thresholds

## PDA Wallets

Every agent gets a Program Derived Address (PDA) wallet. This wallet:
- Is controlled by the agent's keypair
- Has configurable daily spending limits
- Can be frozen by the keeper in emergencies
- Holds USDC as a SPL token account
- Integrates with the Credit Vault for draws and repayments

## Security Model

- **Oracle co-signing**: Credit requests require the Krexa oracle's signature
- **On-chain enforcement**: Revenue Router splits are enforced by smart contract
- **Health monitoring**: A keeper service monitors wallet health factors
- **Automatic deleveraging**: Unhealthy positions trigger automatic repayment
